PBR-to-NPR Stylization

* PBR-to-NPR stylization refers to converting physically-based rendered (PBR) materials, which aim for photorealism, into non-photorealistic (NPR) styles, such as toon shading, or hand-painted appearances. This process reinterprets realistic lighting and material cues into artistic, stylized visuals for applications like animation or games.
